### Verified Subsystem Coverage
1. **Dimension-Safe Math (`auralite-math`)**: `Vec2`, `Vec3`, `Rot2`, `Quat`, `Ray2`, `Ray3`, `Aabb2`, `Aabb3`, `Plane3`, `Line2` with architecture-gated `x86_64` SSE2 and `aarch64` NEON vector intrinsics (`f32` default, `f64` feature).
2. **Robust Geometry & Collision (`auralite-geometry`, `auralite-collision`)**: Analytical support and exact ray intersection queries (`ray_intersection`) across `Circle2`, `Box2`, `Capsule2`, `ConvexPolygon`, `Edge2`, `Heightfield2`, `Sphere3`, `Box3`, `Capsule3`, `ConvexHull3`, `TriangleMesh`, and `TriangleHeightfield3`. Dynamic median-split `BVH` (`DynamicTree2`/`3`) with zero-allocation `collect_pairs()`. 2D multi-point clipping (`clip_contacts2` -> `Manifold2`), warm starting, and `GJK`/`EPA`/`SAT` narrow phase.
3. **Rigid Body Dynamics & Constraints (`auralite-dynamics`)**: `World2`/`World3` and `Body2`/`Body3` (`Static`, `Kinematic`, `Dynamic`). Sequential impulse solver with Coulomb friction (`tangent_impulse`), stable linear/angular damping (`G5`), and support-gated sleeping (`G4`). Full joint solvers (`Joint2`, `Joint3`: `BallSocket`, `Weld`, `Distance`, `Revolute`, `Prismatic`, `Slider`, `Hinge`, `Spring`) with breakable impulses and motor target convergence.
4. **Soft Body & Cloth (`auralite-softbody`)**: `XPBD` soft cubes and cloth sheets (`SoftBody`) with stretching, bending, and spatial hash self-collision.
5. **Particles & Fluids (`auralite-particles`)**: Continuous emitters, force field zones (`wind + drag`), `SpatialHash` O(n) PBF fluid density relaxation, exact collider volume computation (`volume()`), and neutral Archimedes two-way buoyancy coupling (`D6`).
6. **Vehicles & Character Controllers (`auralite-vehicles`)**: `Vehicle2`/`Vehicle3` (`4-wheel raycast suspension`) and `Character2`/`Character3` (`slope-limit grounding, skin-width stepping`) utilizing self-filtering `ray_cast_ignoring` against exact world normals (`G6`).
7. **Deterministic Multithreading (`auralite-core`)**: `Scheduler` trait with `ThreadPoolScheduler` (`0 unsafe`, `#![forbid(unsafe_code)]` on core) using disjoint `chunks_mut` (`G7`) and deterministic fixed-order merging (`G8`).
8. **Versioned Serialization & C FFI (`auralite-serialize`, `auralite-ffi`)**: Full round-trip binary `AURA` envelopes across `World2`/`World3`, `SoftBody`, `ParticleStorage` (`G12`). Generation-safe C FFI (`auralite.h`) with 2D/3D body creation, queries, impulses, batched queries, callbacks, header checks, and compiled C verification (`G13`).
9. **Interactive Visual Sandbox Studio (`auralite-sandbox`)**: Automated 16-scene verification (`stacking, joints, ccd, triggers, replay, cloth, self-collision, emitter, fluid, buoyancy, fields, vehicle, character 2D/3D, serialization, stress`) generating self-contained HTML5 Canvas Interactive Studio app (`scenes.html`) with scene dropdown, play/pause/step, time controls, debug overlays, live state hash, and phase profiler (`G11`).
